Official School Logo

Symbolism & Meaning

The logo is set inside a ruby-shaped diamond to represent the Ruby Jubilee of St. Antony's Public School, celebrating 40 years of growth and excellence.

The circular emblem at the center stands for unity and the school community. The open book symbolizes knowledge and continuous learning, while the Alpha and Omega (A and Ω) signify God as the beginning and the end, grounding our education in faith.

The year 1986 marks the establishment of the school, and the surrounding text highlights the identity and legacy of St. Antony's Public School, Anakkal.

Key Elements:

  • Ruby-shaped diamond (40th Jubilee)
  • Circular emblem for unity
  • Open book for knowledge
  • Alpha and Omega for faith
