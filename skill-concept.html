Terrasense Project

Disaster Monitoring & Early Warning System

In the Terrasense project, I played a central role in conceptual development, research, and technical structuring. I contributed to identifying early landslide warning indicators such as infrasonic sound detection and environmental signal monitoring, and helped design the system framework, block diagrams, and presentation flow. I also represented the project during exhibitions and evaluations, clearly communicating its real-world disaster management applications.

As an achievement, Terrasense secured 1st Prize at the exhibition level and later progressed as a startup initiative, earning recognition and opportunities for higher-level discussions, including engagement related to a Central Government contract.

Key Features

  • Infrasonic sensing framework
  • Environmental signal monitoring
  • Early-warning system structuring
  • Positioned for national-level collaboration
