Problem Solving

Problem solving is at the core of my approach to design and technology. I believe that every challenge is an opportunity to innovate. Whether it's diagnosing a complex technical bug, structuring a new project model, or finding the most efficient way to communicate a high-level concept visually, I rely on a combination of logical deduction and creative exploration.

The Framework

My methodology involves a multi-step process:

  • Discovery & Research: Deep-diving into the problem space to understand all variables and constraints.
  • Deconstruction: Breaking down massive, overwhelming hurdles into small, solvable units.
  • Hypothesis & Testing: Rapidly iterating through potential solutions and validating results.
  • Implementation: Deploying the most robust solution and documenting the process for future reference.

Systems Thinking

I don't just solve for the immediate bug; I solve for the system. By identifying root causes and environmental signals—as seen in my work with the Terrasense early warning system—I ensure that solutions are sustainable and scalable. This mindset allows me to bridge the gap between technical complexity and user-centric design.

Core Competencies

  • Strategic Project Structuring
  • Technical Troubleshooting
  • Creative Solution Design
  • Framework Optimization
