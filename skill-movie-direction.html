Director's Role

In the school documentary project, I served as the Director, overseeing the overall concept, storytelling structure, and visual execution. I was responsible for planning the narrative flow, coordinating shoots, guiding interviews, and ensuring the message reflected the school’s history and values accurately.

I supervised editing decisions, frame composition, and background scoring to maintain professional quality. My role required leadership, time management, and creative decision-making to deliver a documentary that was both meaningful and visually engaging.
