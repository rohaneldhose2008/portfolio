Graphic Design

In graphic design, I work with tools such as Affinity Designer, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator to create visually appealing and meaningful designs. I focus on clarity, balance, and aesthetics by applying design principles like the Golden Ratio, ensuring that the final design is both attractive and easy for viewers to understand. My aim is to communicate ideas effectively through thoughtful composition, typography, and visual hierarchy.
